02 — Methodology

Hiring starts the same way.

With a brief I actually understand. Before I post a single job or search a single name, I sit with the hiring manager and get specific — what success looks like in 90 days, what's failed before, what's non-negotiable.

STAGE 01

Brief

Define 90-day success, past failures, and non-negotiables with the hiring manager.

STAGE 02

Source & Qualify

Sourced wide via LinkedIn Recruiter, boards, and referrals — pre-screened with purpose, not flooded.

STAGE 03

Manage & Close

Full lifecycle ownership — screening, coordinating, advising on offers, handling rejections.

STAGE 04

Measure

Time-to-fill, offer acceptance rate, drop-off points — so the next hire is faster than the last.
